About Bayreach

Who We Are

We are a group of students from Homestead High School who got together in 2018 with the simple goal to make sure students' voices are heard and understood. We felt that there was a huge disconnect between the students and the administration and we wanted to mend that gap.

Our Inspiration

Our fellow students and peers, raising valid concerns, worries, and suggessions to improve the place we practically live in - almost 50 hours a week - push us forward to achieve our goal. We ask ourselves, why should these student voices go unheard? Why should the student body's voice be restricted solely to the few who are selected to represent us?

This is why we have built a platform to allow all student opinions to be heard, with the belief that the student body will be accurately understood. Join us by downloading our app to make our school a better place for the students its meant to serve.
